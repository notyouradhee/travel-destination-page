Overview of Arlington, Texas

Arlington, located between Dallas and Fort Worth, is a vibrant city known for its sports, entertainment, and rich history. It’s home to the famous AT&T Stadium, Six Flags Over Texas, and a bustling arts and culture scene. Arlington offers something for everyone, from thrilling amusement parks to scenic outdoor spaces.

Top Attractions in Arlington, Texas

AT&T Stadium

Home of the Dallas Cowboys, AT&T Stadium is a must-visit for sports fans and offers stadium tours and events year-round.

Six Flags Over Texas

This massive amusement park offers thrilling rides, live entertainment, and family-friendly attractions for visitors of all ages.

Globe Life Park

Former home of the Texas Rangers, Globe Life Park is a historic ballpark that now hosts concerts, events, and community activities.

  • Hilton Arlington: Conveniently located near Six Flags and AT&T Stadium, offering excellent service and comfort.
